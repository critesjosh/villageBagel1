How it works.

I bake most mornings from 6ish-10ish at Mirabelle Restaurant in Avon (right through the Beaver Creek security gates on the left). My customers can buy fresh-out-of-the-oven bagels directly from me here. This is by far the freshest, most delicious way to enjoy Village Bagel. Pick up is behind the main entrance out of the kitchen door of the restaurant (hence "back door bagels"). You can buy any amount when you pick up at the kitchen. I take cash, checks, and cards.
